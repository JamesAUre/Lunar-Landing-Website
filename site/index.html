The space bros


We're pleased you have an interest of who we are. Well, allow us, the space bros, to introduce ourselves. Our names are Neutron and Apollo. We are best friends who met while working at another hotel firm called Griffin. We were both space enthusiasts and we loved to deliver impeccable service. We decided to use all our funds to make our dream a reality of putting space and service together to make Lunar Landing Hotel. We started off making a small hotel orbitting earth in 2054, everyone was thrilled for the opportunity to experience an outer space adventure. Soon, we expanded to other planets in our milky way and now our hotel can be found across all corners of the galaxy. You only need to make one accomodation to stay at any hotel for the time being, why enter a location when you can stay at any location?
